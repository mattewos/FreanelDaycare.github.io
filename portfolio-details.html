At our daycare center, we believe in the importance of connecting children with nature and fostering a love for the outdoors. Our "Nature Explorers" project is a comprehensive initiative that integrates outdoor play, nature-based learning, and environmental education into our daycare curriculum. Through nature walks, field trips, garden activities, and nature-based learning experiences, we provide immersive opportunities for children to develop a deep appreciation for the natural world.
